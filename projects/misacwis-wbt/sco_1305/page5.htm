Web-based Training

As you know, WBT teaches you how to use those MiSACWIS screens and features you'll access most often by providing basic tutoring and hands-on experience performing simulated tasks in a safe environment.

You can access MiSACWIS WBT in the following ways:

  • From the DHS Intranet, navigate to OmniTrack Plus (OTP).
  • From within certain help topics, click the WBT icon.
