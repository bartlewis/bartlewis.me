Policy links

MiSACWIS provides Web-based access to relevant policy guidance to help you make decisions that rely on state laws and policies.

You access policy guidance from inside MiSACWIS. Just click help & training in the header, and then select Rules, Regulations & Policy. You can also access policy guidance from screen-level topics within online help.
